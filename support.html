Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get plant recommendations?
Plant Companion provides personalized recommendations based on your garden profile. Make sure to complete your garden profile with your location (hardiness zone), soil type, and sun exposure for the best recommendations.
Why isn't the weather information showing?
Weather information requires location permissions. Please go to your device Settings → Plant Companion → Location and enable location access. The app uses your location to provide local weather data for better plant care suggestions.
How do I add plants to my garden?
You can search for plants using the search feature, browse recommendations, or manually add plants through the garden planning feature. Once added, you can track their care, journal entries, and growth progress.
Is my data stored online?
Your plant data and garden information are stored locally on your device. We only use your location (with permission) to fetch weather data and provide recommendations. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
How do I update my garden profile?
Navigate to the Profile section in the app to update your garden zone, soil type, sun exposure, and other settings. Changes to your profile will update your plant recommendations automatically.
Can I use the app without location access?
Yes, but some features will be limited. You can manually set your hardiness zone in your garden profile, but weather information and location-based recommendations won't be available without location permissions.
